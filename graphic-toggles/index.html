A flexible and versatile way to support the mission of the AGA Research Foundation is to leave a gift in your will or living trust, known as a charitable bequest.

By including a bequest to the AGA Research Foundation in your will or living trust, you are ensuring that we can continue to fund talented investigators for years to come.

Meet with your attorney and simply revise the bequest language so that your gift to the AGA Research Foundation is a percentage of your estate instead of a specific amount.
